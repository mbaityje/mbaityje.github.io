Elevator pitch

I am a Group Leader at Eawag, a research institute of the ETH domain (Switzerland). With my group, I use methods from machine learning and statistical physics to approach problems mainly related to water systems. In addition, I am interested in understanding the dynamics of high-dimensional systems (such as deep neural networks or glasses) in the presence of a large number of constraints.
In addition to my institutional research topics, I have a personal concern about how science, as an instrument of knowledge and power, can be used both to improve our lives and to perpetrate injustice.
