Marco Baity-Jesi

I am a theoretical physicist and the leader of the Machine Learning & Complex Systems Group at Eawag, the Swiss Federal Institute of Aquatic Science and Technology.

My work spans fundamental questions in machine learning and applied work on environmental challenges, with a strong interest in making scientific work useful for society.
